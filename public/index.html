About Me

I am a 2nd year PhD student at Télécom Paris (Institut Polytechnique de Paris) under the supervision of Alasdair Newson, Yann Gousseau, and Andrés Almansa. My research interests are video / image editing, data generation, deep learning, and computer vision. Before that, I worked at Gleamer on bone fracture detection in x-ray images. I graduated from Télécom Paris, and obtained the “Mathématiques, Vision, Apprentissage” (MVA) Master’s degree from ENS Paris-Saclay.
